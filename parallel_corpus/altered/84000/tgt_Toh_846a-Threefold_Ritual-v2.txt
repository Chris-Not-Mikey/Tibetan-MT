The Threefold Ritual

                    

                    
The qualities of the holy Dharma are inconceivable.
The qualities of the noble Saṅgha are inconceivable.
The unsurpassed fruition of true faith In the inconceivable Three Jewels is inconceivable.

                    
Now that you have taken such a rare embodiment endowed with the freedoms, Use the energy of your magical abilities and faith to approach, And listen to the profound teaching of the Victor In this sanctuary bedecked with the jewels of liberation.

                    
we dedicate it So that you may increase in splendor and retinue and accomplish what you desire.

                    
This concludes “The Threefold Ritual.”
